# Privacy Policy for InTune

**Last updated: April 15, 2026**

## Overview

InTune ("the App") is a guitar utility application that provides a guitar tuner, metronome, and chord library. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information.

## Data Collection

**InTune does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.**

## Microphone / Audio Permission

The App requests access to your device's microphone (`RECORD_AUDIO`) solely to detect the pitch of guitar strings in real time. Audio is processed entirely on-device and is never recorded, stored, or sent anywhere.

## Local Storage

The App stores your preferences (theme, guitar type, metronome settings) locally on your device using Android's SharedPreferences API. This data never leaves your device.

## Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or data-sharing services.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Policy

Any updates to this policy will be reflected on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
